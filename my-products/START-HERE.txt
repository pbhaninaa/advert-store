========================================
  NWABISILE SHOP — ADD YOUR PRODUCTS
========================================

Everything goes in this folder on GitHub:

  my-products/
    products.json     ← your product list
    images/           ← your product photos

HOW TO UPDATE THE LIVE WEBSITE (GitHub)
---------------------------------------
1. Open your shop on GitHub in a browser:
   https://github.com/pbhaninaa/advert-store

2. Open the folder:  my-products

3. Add or replace photos:
   - Open  my-products/images
   - Click  Add file  →  Upload files
   - Drag your photos into the page
   - Click  Commit changes

4. Update the product list:
   - Open  my-products/products.json
   - Click the pencil icon (Edit)
   - Change names, prices, sizes, colours
   - Click  Commit changes

5. Wait 1–2 minutes, then open your shop:
   https://pbhaninaa.github.io/advert-store/

IMPORTANT
---------
- The "image" field in products.json must match the photo filename exactly.
- Each product needs a unique "id" number (1, 2, 3 …).
